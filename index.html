About me

My name is Oscar and the puppy in the image is my future dog. I was born and raised in Leon, Nicaragua. For those who don't know, it's a small and beautiful country in Central America. I grew up in a medium sized family and I am the youngest of four brothers and sisters.

Growing up I used to play a lot of different sports like: basketball, soccer, baseball, boxing and taekwondo. Don't worry I am not lethal.

I graduated as an Industrial Engineer after obtaining a scholarship playing basketball in college. My hobbies are: working out, playing basketball, playing guitar and spending time with my family.

I currently work as a Regional Customer Service Manager / Account Manager for a software company based in Johannesburg. I have a fascination about technology and it is the reason why I decided to enroll in the coding botcamp at Georgia Tech.
